About College

Welcome To Government Polytechnic Mohammadi Kheri

Government Polytechnic Mohammadi, Kheri was established in 2017. It offers 3 courses: Computer Science & Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. The college is AICTE approved and affiliated to BTEUP. Fully furnished labs with internet and projection facilities.
