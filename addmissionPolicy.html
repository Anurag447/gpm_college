ADDMISSION POLICY
Admission in the Institute are taken as per guidelines given by JEECUP and BTEUP. Candidates get admission in Polytechnics through counselling after qualifying the Entrance Examination conducted by Joint Entrance Examination Council (JEECUP), Lucknow. All the reservations are effective as per UP government rules. For Courses, Qualification & other details refer tohttps://www.jeecup.nic.in
Entrance Examination (JEECUP) from filling Months : January and Fabruary Every Year
Entrance Examination Date : Last week of april
Result of Entrance Examination : Mid of May
Counselling for admission : June and July
Documents required :
Following documents are required for first year student Admission:
  • Allotment letter generated by JEECUP counselling.
  • Fee draft in favour of Government Polytechnic, Mohammadi Kheri payable at college.
  • Self-attested photocopies of educational qualification certificates such as high school, intermediate, graduation, etc. and other such as Aadhar card, Caste certificate, Income certificate and Domicile certificate.
  • Original transfer certificate, migration certificate, character certificate of last institution and medical fitness certificate from CMO of any government district hospital.
  • Gap certificate. (if any)
Document Rentention Policy :
We do not submit any original educational certificate at the time of admission.
